Rider Agreement – Big Red Connect

By riding with Big Red Connect, you agree to the following policies. These are displayed in-vehicle, on our website, and shared at time of booking:

  • Payment: Due as agreed. Missed or late payment may result in refusal of service.
  • Cancellation: Cancel at least 3 hours in advance for refund eligibility.
  • No-Shows: $5 fee after 5-minute wait.
  • Stops/Changes: May result in a $5 fee per extra stop or major detour.
  • Damage or Mess: Fees up to $200 may apply for required cleaning or repairs.
  • Group Responsibility: The booking party is responsible for the behavior and costs of all group members.
  • Insurance Disclaimer: Rides are offered by an independent operator under a personal auto policy with a rideshare endorsement. This is not a licensed commercial or taxi service. Limited liability applies.
  • Dash Cam Notice: Audio and video may be recorded inside the vehicle for safety and liability purposes. Riding implies consent.
  • Voluntary Participation: All rides are voluntary and at your own risk. Big Red Connect is not liable for loss, injury, or damages unless caused by gross negligence or willful misconduct.
  • Travel Fairness Rule: A flat travel fee may apply for pickups outside the local service area — $10 for 1–15 miles beyond, $20 for 16 miles or more. Long trips that already cover travel time and mileage do not incur additional fees.
  • Loyalty Consistency: If you receive a quoted fare (e.g., $10 local) and request a return trip within the same night, that same rate will normally be honored for fairness and consistency. However, if the return trip occurs during a special rate period (Rush Hour, Airport, or Special Event / Concert traffic), the applicable base rate for that period may apply. You will always be quoted up front before confirming your return ride.
By accepting a ride, you agree to these terms.
No physical signature is required for single or routine rides.
