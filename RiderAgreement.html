  • Group Responsibility: Booking party is liable for actions of all riders in their group.
  • Insurance Disclaimer: Rides are offered by an independent operator under a personal policy with a rideshare endorsement. Riders acknowledge that coverage is limited compared to standard commercial carriers.
  • Limitation of Liability: By riding with Big Red Connect, the rider voluntarily assumes all risks associated with transportation services. The company and driver are not liable for personal injury, property loss, or damage except in cases of gross negligence or intentional misconduct.
