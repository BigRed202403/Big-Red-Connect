Rider Agreement

Transparency, safety, and fairness — the Big Red Connect way.

By riding with Big Red Connect, you agree to the following policies. These terms are displayed in-vehicle, on the website, and shared when booking.

  • Payment: Due as agreed. Missed or late payments may result in refusal of service.
  • Cancellation: Cancel at least 3 hours in advance for refund eligibility.
  • No-Shows: A $5 fee applies after a 5-minute wait.
  • Stops/Changes: A $5 fee applies for each extra stop or significant detour.
  • Damage or Mess: Cleaning or repair fees of up to $200 may apply.
  • Group Responsibility: The booking party is responsible for the conduct and costs of all group members.
  • Insurance Disclaimer: Rides are offered by an independent operator under a personal auto policy with a rideshare endorsement. This is not a licensed commercial taxi service. Limited liability applies.
  • Dash Cam Notice: Audio and video may be recorded inside the vehicle for safety and liability purposes. Riding implies consent.
  • Voluntary Participation: All rides are voluntary and at your own risk. Big Red Connect is not liable for loss, injury, or damages unless caused by gross negligence or willful misconduct.
  • Travel Fairness Rule: A flat travel fee may apply for pickups outside the local service area — $10 for 1–15 miles beyond and $20 for 16 miles or more. Long trips that already cover travel time and mileage do not incur extra fees.
  • Loyalty Consistency: If you receive a quoted fare, such as a $10 local ride, and request a return trip within the same night, that same rate will normally be honored for fairness and consistency. However, if the return occurs during a special-rate period, including Rush Hour, Airport, or a Special Event, the applicable base rate may apply. You will always be quoted before confirming the return ride.
By accepting a ride, you agree to these terms.
No physical signature is required for standard or recurring rides.
