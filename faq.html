Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you plan your connection.

How do I get around with Big Red?

Just text me at 405-378-4024 and I’ll let you know if I’m available.
No app, no surge pricing — just local, friendly service.

How much does it cost?

Standard rides start at $10 and cover the first 10 miles.
Miles 11–20 are $1.25 per mile, and miles beyond 20 are $1.00 per mile.

Rush Hour rides start at $15. Airport and Special Event rides start at $20.

Travel Fairness Rule: A travel fee only applies when a short, local trip is requested far from my current driving area or home zone. Long-distance trips that already fairly cover time and mileage do not receive an additional travel fee.

Do you record video inside the vehicle?

Yes — for safety and accountability. Dash cams record both inside and outside the vehicle. Riders are notified by the in-car display and in the Rider Agreement.

Do I need to sign anything?

First-time riders agree to a one-time Rider Agreement. You do not need to sign again unless something changes or the ride is connected to a special event.

Where do you operate?

Local Zone:
Oklahoma City · Moore · Norman · Midwest City · Del City

Extended Coverage:
Yukon · Mustang · Edmond · Noble · Newcastle · Tuttle · Harrah · Choctaw · Jones · Luther · Far East Norman · East Norman

Outer Coverage:
Shawnee · Tecumseh · Guthrie · Blanchard · Wellston · Seminole · Chickasha · Goldsby · Lexington · Purcell · and beyond.

Trips outside these areas are evaluated by distance. When a longer route already fairly covers travel time and mileage, no additional travel fee applies.

Can I bring food or drinks?

Sure — just be respectful and tidy. If you need help carrying something, I’ve got you.
