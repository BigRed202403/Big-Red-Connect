Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get around with Big Red?

Just text me at 833-526-2643 and I’ll let you know if I’m available. No app, no hassle—just local, friendly service.

How much does it cost?

Rates start at $10, with commute hours at $15 and events at $20. If you're traveling more than 10 miles, it's $1.25 per mile after that. I’ll confirm pricing when you text.

Do you record video inside the vehicle?

Yes, for safety and accountability. A dash cam records both outside and inside. Riders are notified by a digital sign and in the terms linked on the site.

Do I need to sign anything?

First-time riders sign a one-time agreement. You don’t need to sign again unless something changes.

Where do you operate?

I serve Moore, OKC, Norman, Mustang, Yukon, Edmond, Piedmont, Shawnee, Tecumseh, and nearby areas.

Can I bring food or drinks?

Sure—just be respectful and tidy. If you need help carrying something, I’ve got you.
