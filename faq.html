Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get around with Big Red?

Just text me at 405-378-4024 and I’ll let you know if I’m available. No app, no surge pricing—just local, friendly service.

How much does it cost?

Standard rides start at $10 (includes up to 10 miles).
Longer trips are $1.25 per mile for miles 11–20 and $1.00 per mile beyond 20.

Rush Hour and Airport base fares are $15, and Special Events are $20.

Travel Fairness Rule: A $10 travel fee may apply for pickups 1–15 miles outside my local area, or $20 for 16+ miles. Long trips already covering distance don’t incur this fee.

Do you record video inside the vehicle?

Yes, for safety and accountability. Dash cams record both inside and outside the vehicle. Riders are notified by in-car display and in the Rider Agreement.

Do I need to sign anything?

First-time riders agree to a one-time Rider Agreement. You don’t need to sign again unless something changes or it’s for a special event.

Where do you operate?

Local Zone (no travel fee): Moore, Norman, Oklahoma City, Midwest City, Del City, Mustang, and Yukon.

Extended Coverage: Shawnee, Harrah, Edmond, Guthrie, El Reno (outer), Tuttle, Purcell, Seminole, and Stillwater.

*Flat $10 or $20 travel fee may apply outside the Local Zone.*

Can I bring food or drinks?

Sure—just be respectful and tidy. If you need help carrying something, I’ve got you.
