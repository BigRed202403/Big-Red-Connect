Info & Policies

Fare Schedule

  • Base Rate: $10 (up to 10 miles)
  • 11–20 miles: $1.25 per mile
  • Beyond 20 miles: $1.00 per mile
  • Rush Hour Base: $15
  • Airport Base: $15
  • Special Event Base: $20

Travel Fairness Rule

A travel fee may apply only when:

  • The pickup is 1–15 miles outside the OKC Metro area ($10 fee).
  • The pickup is more than 15 miles outside the OKC Metro area ($20 fee).
  • The pickup is local but far from my current driving zone (e.g., Moore-based but picking up in Edmond for a short Edmond-to-Edmond trip).

Long-distance trips that already fairly cover time and mileage (e.g., Edmond-to-Norman) do not incur a travel fee.

Terms of Service

  • Payment is due as agreed. Late or missed payments may result in service refusal.
  • No-shows are charged a $5 fee after 5 minutes.
  • Extra stops may add $5 per stop.
  • Cleaning or damage fees may apply (up to $200).
  • Group leader = group responsibility.
  • Dash cameras record inside for safety and liability protection.
  • All rides are voluntary and at the rider’s own risk.
