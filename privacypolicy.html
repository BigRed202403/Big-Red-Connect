Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

At Big Red Connect, your privacy and trust are very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you connect with us.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide (such as your phone number, name, and email) when you text us, scan QR codes, or interact via our website or social media.
  • Information related to your communications with us to provide safe, reliable local connections.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and coordinate your local ride connections.
  • To send you updates and important messages based on your consent.
  • To improve our services and maintain communication.

Consent to Receive Text Messages

By texting Big Red Connect or scanning our QR codes, you consent to receive text messages related to your inquiries, confirmations, and service updates. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can opt out anytime by replying “STOP.” We do not send marketing or promotional texts without your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our communication and data collection are limited to providing safe, local, and voluntary connections.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information is used solely for coordinating and maintaining ride connections requested by you.

Your Rights

You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying “STOP.” For other inquiries about your data, contact us at bigredconnectokc@icloud.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The most current version will always be available on our website with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: bigredconnectokc@icloud.com
